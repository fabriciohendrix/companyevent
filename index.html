Bikepark Albstadt

Concept: A day in one of the most iconic places for the modern MTB, where every year pro riders face each other in the official UCI XCC and XCO World Cup stage.

Bikepark Albstadt has 6 constantly maintained routes with individual lines in different levels of difficulty. With a drag lift, riders can relax and let themselves be pulled to the top of the downhill route.

Suitable for all levels.

For beginners, all jumps can be bypassed without any problems. Full-face helmets and protectors are required (chest, back, knee and elbow). In case you don't have those, there's how to rent in loco.

Information:
Arrival

There are regional trains from Stuttgart Hauptbahnhof and it takes around 1h40min to get there. Since it's a regional train, we can transport bikes if space is available. By car it's a 1h20min trip.

Food

There's a restaurant inside the bikepark and since it's located in the city center we can also find other options.
